Bring VR to Your Class

We have the headsets and support to help you start

The XR Lab can set up quick, low-friction VR activities that connect to your course goals. We'll handle hardware, safety, and logistics - you focus on learning outcomes.

Quick ways to use VR

  • 5-10 minute demo stations during class
  • Rotating small-group explorations
  • Prebuilt creativity and sketch tools
  • Guided field trips and exhibits

We'll help you plan

  • Pick a relevant experience for your topic
  • Schedule the headsets and room flow
  • Set clear prompts and reflection questions

Curious to try VR with your class? Contact the Research & Innovation Center to reserve headsets and plan a session.
