💡 Bring the Good Vibes to Your Classroom

Part of the Research & Innovation Center

The Vibe Board brings collaboration and creativity to the classroom — helping faculty share ideas, present content, and connect students through interactive technology.

  • 🖊️ Use the stylus to write, draw, and annotate over slides or web pages
  • 📺 Instantly cast or mirror your laptop, tablet, or phone screen
  • 📊 Display live dashboards, presentations, and interactive web tools
  • 🌍 Integrate 360° and WebXR content for immersive learning
  • 🎮 Pair with the XR Lab for hands-on experiences

Faculty can now check out and reserve the Vibe Board for class sessions — ask about scheduling time through the Research & Innovation Center.
