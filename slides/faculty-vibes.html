Use the Vibe Board in Your Class

From the Research & Innovation Center

The Vibe Board is a large, touch-enabled display you can use to present, annotate, cast devices, and run interactive web tools with your students.

  • Write, draw, and annotate over slides or web pages with the stylus
  • Instantly cast or mirror your laptop, tablet, or phone
  • Show live dashboards, presentations, and interactive web apps
  • Pair with the XR Lab for hands-on activities

Bring it to your class

  • Share slides, annotate live, or explore XR content with students
