Lab Etiquette & Getting Started

New to VR? Start here. Be safe, be kind, and have fun.

Getting Started

  • Check in with staff before using headsets.
  • Ask for fit help: adjust straps, IPD, and clarity.
  • Start with low‑motion apps if you’re new (try Bait!).
  • Mind the boundary and look around before moving.

Hygiene & Safety

  • Use wipes between users; let gear dry fully.
  • Report discomfort or nausea—take breaks often.
  • No food/drinks near equipment; keep cables clear.
  • Stop immediately if you feel unwell or disoriented.

Lab Etiquette

  • Be mindful of others’ space and noise.
  • Share time fairly during busy periods.
  • Return controllers and straps to holders when done.
  • Ask before changing app/library settings.

Need Help?

  • Staff can recommend apps for comfort and skill level.
  • We can demo guardian setup and recentering.
  • Interested in projects or research? Ask us!
  • Have feedback? We’d love to hear it.

Tip: If you’re sensitive to motion, try seated apps first.
